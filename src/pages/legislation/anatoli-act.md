# Table of Contents

# I. Short Name

1. This act may be referred to as the Anatoli Act, 2022

# II. Division of Territory

1. The territory of the City State of Anatoli shall be made into divisions, all of which shall operate as separate cities.
2. All territory referred to in the Mainland Shiftania Transfer of Sovereignty Act, 2022 as well as any extension to this land, shall be a new city called “Vremya”.
    1. The Russian language shall continue to operate as a regional language of the Territory.
3. The territory comprising all built property north of the Iverycity border, exclusive of all territory referred to in the Mainland Shiftania Transfer of Sovereignty Act, 2022, and inclusive of property built before the Fifteenth of April, in the Year of our Lord, twenty twenty two shall be a new city called “Anatoli”.
    1. This city may also be referred to as “Anatoli City”.
4. All existing territory south of the Iverycity border shall be a new city called “Wairua”.

# III. Creation of Confederation

1. All cities referred to in article II of this act shall form one country by the name of “Anatoli”.
2. The capital of Anatoli shall be Anatoli City.

# IV. The Sovereign

1. All executive power shall be vested in one Soverign of Anatoli.
    1. The Sovereign shall act in accordance with this Constitutional Act.
    2. All powers not granted in this Act as a reserve power shall be performed by and with the advice of the Sovereign’s Government, or its Ministers, or under authorization by an Act of Parliament.
2. Chances to the line of succession shall be provided for by an Act of Parliament.
3. The Sovereign shall reserve the right to name a Prime Minister after a Parliamentary Election.
4. A Parliamentary Election shall be fixed on a day to be chosen by the Sovereign, but shall not exceed more than four (4) years after the previous election.
5. The Sovereign shall cause their Assent to be granted to an Act of Parliament after such an Act has been passed in identical form by both Houses of Parliament.
    1. The Sovereign shall be authorized by reserve power to withhold Assent to an Act of Parliament, or to repeal such Assent, if such Act shall be contrary to this Constitutional Act.

# V. The Legislature

1. There shall be a National Parliament, which shall consist of a lower House of Commons and a Senate.
    1. The Commons shall be populated by elected members chosen exclusively by the people of Anatoli.
    2. The Senate shall be selected by the Sovereign, by and with the advice of the Government.
2. The National Parliament shall be responsible for all matters affecting all citizens of Anatoli, including:
    1. Foreign affairs
    2. Criminal matters
    3. Marriage
    4. National spending and loaning
    5. The federal tax
    6. Trade and commerce
    7. National defence
    8. Borders and border control
    9. The postal service
    10. Citizenship
    11. Bankruptcy
    12. Patents
    13. Copyright
3. All acts created by the City State of Anatoli and created after the passage of this act by the National Parliament of Anatoli shall be deemed to extend to the Union.
4. No person holding a government office shall bear the citizenship of another State, nor shall they bear allegiance to another State.

# VI. The Judiciary

1. The Judiciary shall consist of one Supreme Court of Anatoli, as well as any inferior court that may be authorized by this Act, or any other Act of Parliament.
    1. The Supreme Court shall have appellate authority over an inferior Court.
    2. The Supreme Court shall appeal to the Supreme Court of Legal District.
2. It is the duty of the Judiciary to adjudicate legal disputes brought forth under the jurisdiction of the law of this land.
3. All Courts shall have the right to authorize relief to a party wronged by an unlawful act.
4. The Courts shall be granted the right to declare an Act or part of an Act null and void to the extent that they may violate this Act, and to authorize relief for any such violation. This Power shall extend to all Courts authorized by section 17 of this Act.

# VII. Freedoms

1. All rights and freedoms as described in Server Law are hereby confirmed and shall be inviolable.

# VIII. Amendment to This Act

1. An Amendment to this Constitutional Act shall be proclaimed to be authorized by the Sovereign where such Amendment has been passed in identical form by a majority vote of sixty (60) per cent of each house of Parliament, and after having received a majority vote of fifty-five (55) per cent of the population in a referendum to be held no later than one hundred and twenty (120) days after the passage of an amendment by the Parliament of Anatoli.
2. An Amendment concerning the modification of State territory shall be proclaimed to be authorized by an ordinary Act of Parliament, where such Act is authorized by
    1. Any affected subdivision; and;
    2. International law, in the case of an international land claim.
